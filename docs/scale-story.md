# Scale Story

## One product today

Start with Neutrogena Hydro Boost Water Gel / Hydro Boost hydration line as the proof point. Build product truth, prompt demand, claim mappings, answer fragments, structured data, and public hosting readiness.

## All Neutrogena hydration products next

Extend the same product truth schema to the broader Hydro Boost and hydration portfolio. Add variant-level labels, ingredients, usage guidance, claims, source URLs, and approval metadata.

## All skincare categories later

Expand into cleansers, acne care, sun care, anti-aging, sensitive skin, and routine-based content. Maintain claim governance at the fragment level.

## Extend across Kenvue brands

Use the same pattern for:

- Aveeno oat science
- Tylenol HCP and consumer education
- Listerine oral care
- Other priority brand and commerce experiences

## Connect enterprise systems

Production scale can connect:

- Salsify for product information
- Aprimo for assets
- approved claims systems
- evidence and substantiation libraries
- CMS publishing workflows
- Shopify storefronts
- retailer syndication payloads

## Add localization and market approvals

Each market and language should have approval metadata, allowed channels, required warnings, legal review status, and final source references.

## Use agents for operating leverage

Agents can support prompt mining, claim mapping, answer drafting, compliance validation, structured data checks, CMS publishing support, and observability. Human approval remains required for regulated or brand-sensitive content.
