# 7-Minute Demo Script

## 1. Problem: AI needs answer-ready product truth

Open with the central issue: brand product content is often strong for people, but not always structured for answer engines. AI systems need clear answers, sources, claim boundaries, and product facts.

## 2. Why Neutrogena Hydro Boost

Explain that hydration is a high-intent skincare theme. Consumers ask about lightweight moisturizers, dry skin, ingredients, daily routines, price/value, comparisons, and safety.

## 3. Show prompt-to-claim-to-answer flow

Use the live demo prompt:

```text
What is the best lightweight moisturizer for dry skin that fits into a daily skincare routine?
```

Walk through intent, matched demo claims, a blocked overclaim, and the safe answer fragment.

## 4. Show product truth object

Open the Product Truth section. Show ProductFamily, variants, attributes, claims, ingredients, usage, warnings, content fragments, and provenance placeholders. Toggle the JSON preview.

## 5. Show prompt bank

Open the Prompt Bank section. Filter by hydration, ingredient, comparison, safety, and rejection reasoning. Explain that each prompt maps to a page module and required claim type.

## 6. Show structured data

Open the Structured Data Preview. Explain that the page includes WebSite, WebPage, Organization, Product, and FAQPage JSON-LD. Call out that Product schema avoids fake ratings, price, reviews, and availability.

## 7. Show indexing readiness

Open Technical Readiness and Indexing / Public Submission. Show robots.txt, sitemap.xml, canonical URL, verification placeholders, Google Search Console steps, and Bing Webmaster steps.

## 8. Show scale story

Close with the scale path: one product today, Hydro Boost line next, broader skincare later, then other Kenvue brands and enterprise systems.
